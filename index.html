Smoothie Bowl

This frozen fruits smoothie bowl is very easy to make and is rich in nutrients.

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup organic frozen mixed beries
  • 1 cup frozen bananas
  • 2 cup frozen almonds
  • 1 mixed fruits
  • 1 scoop vanila protein powder
  • 1 cup granola

Direction:

  1. Add frozen bananas and mixed beries to a blender
  2. Blend on until small bites remain
  3. Add almond milk and protein powder
  4. Blend on low again until the mixter reaches a soft serve consitency
  5. Scoop into 1-2 serve bowls
  6. Toped with mixed fruits and granola
    • Bananas
    • Mangoes
    • Strawberries
    • Blue berries
Benefits:
Rich in fibre
Rich in protein
